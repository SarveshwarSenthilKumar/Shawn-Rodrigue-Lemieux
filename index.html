About Shawn

The remarkable journey of a chess prodigy who became a grandmaster, entrepreneur, and global ambassador for the game

From Chess Prodigy to Grandmaster Entrepreneur

Shawn Rodrigue Lemieux began his chess journey at the tender age of 4, showing an innate talent for the game that would soon blossom into a legendary career. By age 9, he had defeated his first International Master, and at just 12 years old, he became the youngest winner of the Canadian Chess Championship.

His meteoric rise continued as he achieved the Grandmaster title at 16, setting a record as the youngest Canadian to do so. But Shawn's ambitions extended far beyond the chessboard. Recognizing the untapped potential at the intersection of chess, technology, and business, he began building an empire that would revolutionize how the world engages with the game.
