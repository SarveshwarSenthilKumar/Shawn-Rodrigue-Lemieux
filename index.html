About Shawn

The remarkable journey of Canada's chess prodigy who became a grandmaster and tech entrepreneur

From Montreal Prodigy to Chess Visionary

Born in Montreal, Shawn Rodrigue-Lemieux displayed an extraordinary aptitude for chess from the age of 4. By 9, he had defeated his first International Master, and at 12 became the youngest winner of the Canadian Chess Championship—a record that still stands today.

His meteoric rise continued as he achieved the Grandmaster title at just 16 years old, making him the youngest Canadian to ever reach this pinnacle. Shawn's dominance in rapid chess is unparalleled, with three World Rapid Chess Championship titles to his name.

Beyond the board, Shawn has leveraged his strategic mastery to build a chess technology empire, developing platforms that have revolutionized how the game is learned, played, and experienced worldwide.
